How To Play

Getting Started

When you enter the home screen you will see four main options on the left, labeled as "Play", "Stats", "Tutorial", and "Leaderboard".

The "Play" button starts the game.

The "Stats" button displays stats of you and others.

The "Leaderboard" button displays the top 10 players on the game.

The "Sign In" button allows you to sign up into your account for you to record your scores.

Start Playing

Once clicking the "Play" button, you will need to select the difficulty you want to play.

Once you select your difficulty, you will see a map in the right corner. This is where you select your guess

In the top you will see statistics that show your score and time remaining.

Once you select your guess and click the button that you are ready, you will see the screen with your total score in the background and your current score in the foreground.

Once you are done viewing this, you can click the next button to continue playing.
